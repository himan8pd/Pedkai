Technical Glossary

ServiceNow
Industry-standard ITSM (IT Service Management) platform for ticketing and incident workflow.
Netcracker OSS
Operations Support System for service assurance and order management correlation.
EXFO CEM
Customer Experience Management probe system monitoring real-time VoIP/Data quality.
Ribbon SBC 5400
Session Border Controller managing SIP signaling and media (RTP) at the network edge.
AudioCodes OVOC
One Voice Operations Center - a holistic management system for voice network devices and quality of service monitoring.
BroadForward BFX Mgr
Centralized management for Diameter and Signaling controllers, handling routing logic and subscriber policy coordination.
Comverse Insight
Value Added Services (VAS) analytics and management platform for messaging and voicemail traffic monitoring.
Cisco Prime Infra
Unified management for network access, visibility, and control across wired/wireless and EPC core domains.
RH OpenStack (RHOSP)
Red Hat OpenStack Platform, the primary virtualization layer (NFVI) for virtualized RAN and Core.
