## Slide 1 — One-Line Positioning

Pedkai is an AI-Native Operational Reconciliation Engine that restores enterprise truth between CMDB intent and real-world infrastructure behavior.

## Slide 2 — The Problem

- Enterprises run on fragmented operational memory.
- CMDBs drift out of date; telemetry lacks business context.
- Teams lose time debating data accuracy instead of resolving incidents.

## Slide 3 — Why Existing Tools Fall Short

- Discovery tools miss hidden dependencies behind boundaries.
- Dashboards visualize noise but do not reconcile contradictions.
- Generic AIOps often confuses correlation for causation.

## Slide 4 — The Pedkai Solution

- Ingest intent + reality + incident memory.
- Build a Living Context Graph with confidence and lineage.
- Surface policy-safe recommendations for operators and leadership.

## Slide 5 — Core Differentiators

1. **Dark Graph Inference:** proposes hidden edges as hypotheses, then promotes only after multi-source corroboration, structural consistency checks, and policy-gated confidence thresholds.
2. **Abeyance Memory:** persists unresolved clues with wake triggers, revisits them when new evidence arrives, and fuses them into validated findings instead of dropping context.
3. **Telemetry Cross-Examination:** treats manual notes as claims, verifies expected telemetry signatures (or auxiliary evidence in blind zones), and blocks polluted learning signals.
4. **Constitutional Guardrails:** enforces declarative YAML constraints on inference promotion, exposure level, and action eligibility with auditable matched/failed gate traces.
