## Goal

Deliver a high-impact, low-friction demo that makes buyers feel the core value in under 7 minutes:

**Pedkai turns fragmented enterprise records into trusted operational truth.**

- CIO / CTO: strategic risk and modernization return

## Demo Narrative Arc

- Show CMDB says “healthy,” while reality feed shows active degradation.
- Highlight contradictory signals and operator confusion.

"This is the hidden tax of fragmented enterprise memory: everyone has data, nobody has truth."

## Act 2 — The Reconciliation Engine (Aha)

- Reveal Intent vs Reality comparison and confidence scoring.
- Show Dark Graph inference only after corroborating evidence.
- Show Abeyance Memory surfacing previously disconnected clues.

Talk track:
"Pedkai doesn’t guess. It reconciles across sources and proves why each dependency exists."

## Act 3 — Business Impact (Outcome)

- Show resolved drift findings, risk reduction, and faster causal clarity.
- Show policy gate blocking unsafe recommendation.

Talk track:
"You keep your stack. Pedkai injects intelligence and makes existing systems materially smarter."
