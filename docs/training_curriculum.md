# Pedkai NOC Training Curriculum

This document outlines the training modules for NOC staff using the Pedkai Autonomous Network Dashboard.

## Module 1: Introduction to Autonomous Operations
- Understanding the "Human-in-the-loop" philosophy.
- Shift from Alarm Monitoring to Anomaly Validation.
- Role of the AI Agent (Antigravity) and Human Gatekeepers.

## Module 2: Dashboard Navigation & KPI Scorecards
- Interpreting the "Shield Status" and Health Metrics.
- Understanding Value Capture (OpEx savings and Revenue-at-risk).
- Real-time KPI tracking (PRB Utilization, Data Volume).

## Module 3: Incident Lifecycle Management
- Workflow: Anomaly -> SitRep -> Investigation -> Action -> Closed.
- Approving SitReps: Validating AI descriptions against raw alarms.
- Authorizing Actions: Understanding tradeoff rationales and safety constraints.

## Module 4: Service Impact & Topology
- Visualizing the Impact Tree (Upstream/Downstream).
- Identifying Root Cause via Topology Traversal.
- Correlated Alarm Clusters: Noise reduction metrics.

## Module 5: Governance & Security
- Role-Based Access Control (RBAC): Operator vs. Shift Lead permissions.
- Data Sovereignty and Privacy (Consent Management).
- Auditing: Reviewing the decision history and reasoning chains.
